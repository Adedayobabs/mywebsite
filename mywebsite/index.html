Client:
jay young
Company
Jay young is a multifaceted startup brand focused on making profit through forex trade, agriculture and general merchandise sale.
Brief:
i was contacted by the jay young team to create an attactive and vibrant identity which would appeal to their target audience, portraying reliability, accountability and trustworthiness in accordance to their their record.
the main feeling the brand hopes to evoke is happiness and a smile from its customer's
Challenge
create a lasting ad functionnal identity, showing the brand values and remind the audience of the main message of the brand which is happiness.
Process:
Research → Sketch → Design → Review
Research
After several discovery calls and conversation with the client, there was a clear goal of what and we how we wanted the brand to be positioned. I followed that up by doing quantitive and qualitative Research on the industries she would be delving into, how the customer's think, how the brand wants to meet their needs and the unique message that gives the brand an edge in it's market and how best we can leverage on that.
Keywords
happiness, smile, Reliable, Recognizable, Welcoming, Professional, Stable, Memorable
Sketch
The final sketch was a combination of the brand's name and it's main goals.
J+Y+ smile + growth
